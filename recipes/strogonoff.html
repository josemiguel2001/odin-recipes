What is Brazilian Strogonoff?

Strogonoff is a traditional russian dish that mainly consists of beef with a light red sauce and pasta.
Ok! We know that!
But how about "brazilian" strogonoff? It is a delicious and simple adaptation made with chicken alongside cream and ketchup.
Let's learn how to make it!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 3 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp yellow mustard
  • 400g of chicken breasts
  • 200ml cream
  • 3 tbsp chopped green onions
  • 3 garlic cloves

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, heat the olive oil and sauté the onion and garlic, then add the chicken strips
  2. Season with salt and paprika
  3. Sauté and add the mustard alongside the ketchup
  4. Cook for another 1-2 minutes and add the cream (do not boil, just heat up)

What is "pudim"?

Pudim is a beloved Brazilian dessert similar to caramel flan. It’s creamy, smooth, and covered in a glossy layer of caramel syrup. The best part? It’s made with just a few simple ingredients you probably already have at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (395g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 can (using the same can) of milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Blend the condensed milk, milk, eggs, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Pour the caramel syrup (see below) into a round baking pan, tilting it so the bottom is coated.
  4. Pour the mixture over the syrup and cover with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in a water bath for about 1 hour, or until set (a knife should come out clean).
  6. Let cool completely, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours before unmolding.
For the caramel syrup
  1. In a small pan, melt 1 cup of sugar over medium heat, stirring gently until golden brown.
  2. Carefully add ½ cup of hot water (it will bubble!) and stir until smooth.
  3. Let the syrup cool slightly before pouring it into the baking pan.
